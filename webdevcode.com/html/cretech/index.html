About Company

The World’s Automobile Services And Solutions

Auto fixer is a Nigeria based automobile diagnostic company with Australian based highly skilled and experienced and qualified technicians, all our technicians are masters in their fields with qualifications from top colleges in Australia. All our technicians have worked and are still working with the Australian top automotive manufacturers such as, Ford, Honda, Hyundai,vw, Audi, Mercedes, Toyota, Mazda etc. We are focused on making our customers happy at all times with 1year warranty on any repairs done, autofixer is equipped with the latest technology to help do a proper diagnosis on your vehicle.
